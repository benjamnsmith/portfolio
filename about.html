About

My name is Ben and I study computer systems at the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities. I am fascinated by the way our systems are built, from the enterprise intranets to the world wide web as a whole. Technology is changing at a rapid pace now more than ever, and continuing to design apps etc. requires a deep understanding of the underlying systems upon which they're built. I am also interested in their security, and really enjoy the annual USENIX conference, as well as the content posted by Google's Project Zero.

Outside of tech, I'm really interested in languages and cultures of the world. I study Russian and French as well, and am spending my Fall 2022 semester in Montpellier, France.
